How it's built
Construction
Two layers
Body + wing, sliced from the one mark. They recombine pixel-exact at rest — the static logo is untouched.
Pivot
The shoulder
Wing hinges at 51.5% / 28% — where it actually attaches. It lifts up and back, breaking the topline like a real flap.
Cadence
2 beats + rest
0 → 15° → 13° → fold, then a pause. 1.5s calm / 0.9s working. Never a constant frantic loop.
Reduced motion
Soft pulse
Respects prefers-reduced-motion: the wing stays folded and the mark breathes in opacity instead.

One decision to make: is the flap reserved strictly for "Wren is actively doing work," or does it also greet you on load and when Wren has something new to say? I'd keep it to active work — a bird that flaps constantly stops meaning anything.
