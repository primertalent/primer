Privacy Policy

WREN · HIREWREN.COM · EFFECTIVE JUNE 10, 2026 · INTERIM VERSION PENDING LEGAL REVIEW

Wren is an AI recruiting assistant operated by Primer Talent LLC ("we," "us"). This policy explains what data Wren collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. It is written to be read, not skimmed past.

1. Information you provide

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. As you use Wren, you provide information about your recruiting work: candidate resumes, notes, compensation details, job descriptions, client information, call transcripts and summaries, and messages you compose or approve. This is the working data Wren exists to manage.

2. Information about candidates

Wren stores information about job candidates that you, the recruiter, provide. Candidates are not users of Wren, and we process their information on your behalf as part of your recruiting work. You are responsible for having an appropriate basis to share candidate information with service providers like Wren. We do not sell candidate data, use it to train AI models, or share it with other Wren users. If a candidate contacts us requesting deletion of their information, we will honor that request and notify you.

3. Google user data

If you connect your Google account, Wren may access the following, depending on the permissions you grant:

  • Gmail (send): Wren sends emails on your behalf only when you explicitly approve a specific message. Wren cannot read, browse, or modify your inbox with this permission.
  • Google Calendar (read-only): Wren reads your calendar events to detect scheduled interviews, prepare briefing materials, and identify scheduling gaps in your recruiting pipeline.

Wren's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Specifically: Google user data is used only to provide and improve user-facing features of Wren that are visible to you; it is not used for advertising; it is not sold; it is not used to train generalized AI or machine learning models; and humans do not read it except with your explicit consent, for security purposes, to comply with law, or when aggregated and anonymized for internal operations.

You can revoke Wren's access to your Google account at any time at myaccount.google.com/permissions.

4. How AI processing works

Wren uses large language models provided by Anthropic to read, classify, and act on the data described above: screening candidates, drafting messages, extracting details from notes and transcripts, and generating recommendations. Data sent to Anthropic's API is processed under Anthropic's commercial terms and is not used by Anthropic to train its models. AI outputs are suggestions; you review and approve consequential actions.

5. Where data lives

Your data is stored with Supabase (database and authentication) and our application is hosted on Vercel. Data is encrypted in transit. Access to production data is limited to what is necessary to operate and support the service.

6. What we do not do

  • We do not sell your data or candidate data to anyone.
  • We do not use your data to train AI models.
  • We do not share your recruiting data with other Wren users or with employers.
  • We do not serve advertising.

7. Retention and deletion

We retain your data while your account is active. You may request export or deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing the address below. Deletion requests are completed within 30 days. Google user data is deleted when you disconnect your Google account or delete your Wren account, whichever comes first.

8. Changes

If this policy changes materially, we will notify you by email before the change takes effect. This is an interim policy and will be updated following formal legal review; the commitments in sections 3, 6, and 7 will not weaken.

9. Contact

Questions, export requests, or deletion requests: hello@primertalent.com
